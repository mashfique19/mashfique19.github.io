Abstract 
In the rapidly developing field of robotics, human-robot interaction sys
tems are needed to increase the integration of robots into daily human activities. IoT 
and machine learning are being used to identify people based on their facial features. 
Biometric systems record the physical characteristics of the faces in a digital environ
ment and can be used for pattern recognition. One of the behaviors that robots must 
learn to become intelligent is following humans. To interact with humans, robots 
need to be familiar w ith objects, and structures in human living environments. The
main objective of this study is to present the process of developing a usable robotic
tool that can be applied in various fields. Real-time face recognition is the main
goal, and intelligent face recognition technology can transform many industries and
improve the user experience.
